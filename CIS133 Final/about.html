About the Artist

Camille Kofford is a self-taught artist based in Tucson, AZ, working in various mediums and styles. Though not classically trained, she has been creating all her life and continues to learn, grow and experiment for the remainder. She encourages those around her to nurture their own creative sides in whatever way that feels right for them.

The mediums she works with include:

  • Watercolors
  • Oil paints
  • Prismacolor pencils
  • Woodburning
  • Jewlry Making
